flair, without slop

Thirty motifs you can drop in anywhere a page feels too quiet.

Every shape is derived from the existing lattice / hairline / corner-cut language. Use them sparingly: one motif per region, never more than two on a single page. If a shape isn't earning its space, delete it. The point is restraint, not decoration for its own sake.

07 / rules
when in doubt, fewer

A shape that doesn't earn its space is noise.

do
  • →Use one ambient texture per region (page, hero, or card). Layering textures muddies the page.
  • →Pick one accent shape per page (a tag, a stamp, or a side flag). Never two.
  • →Lattice variants always keep at least one peak dot in rust. The peak is the brand signature.
  • →If a shape needs to be explained, delete it.
don't
  • ✕Don't stack shapes from three kits in one region. That's craft theatre, not design.
  • ✕Don't recolor lattice peaks to anything other than rust.
  • ✕Don't animate ambient textures. They're meant to feel like paper, not screensavers.
  • ✕Don't put a corner-cut tag on every card. Once it's everywhere, it's nowhere.
