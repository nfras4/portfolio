Banana, but with measuring tape and graph paper.

Same paper / hairlines / rust system, two new directions. BAN-01 plots a banana on an 8 px lattice as a connected polygon, with the stem and tip as rust anchors. BAN-02 draws the banana as a CAD blueprint: hairline outline, dashed arc centres, dimensioned callouts, JetBrains Mono labels. The joke does the work; the execution stays restrained.

01Concept · Banana on Lattice

Banana, plotted — dot-grid silhouette

BAN-01

A banana whose silhouette is drawn through 8 px lattice intersections. The body is a filled polygon, the lattice shows faintly behind it like graph paper, and two rust nodes pin the stem and the tip — the only chromatic events in the mark. Reads as "we work in spreadsheets, but the spreadsheet is a banana."

02Concept · Banana Blueprint

Banana, dimensioned — CAD spec sheet

BAN-02

The banana as a technical drawing. Hairline outline only, dashed construction lines through the arc centres, measurement brackets along the bounding box, callouts to the stem, apex, and arc origin. JetBrains Mono labels in dim ink with one rust callout (the stem) for the accent. Doubles as the most on-brand version of "Tek Monkeys" — engineering precision applied to a banana.

04Notes · what to look for

BAN-01 · banana on lattice

  • · The banana shape is a 14-vertex polygon, every vertex on an 8 px lattice intersection.
  • · Background lattice dots stay faint so they read as graph paper, not noise.
  • · Rust accents at the stem tip and the right body tip — the two extreme points.
  • · Trade-off: at small sizes the lattice dots merge with the silhouette, so the favicon variant drops them entirely.

BAN-02 · banana blueprint

  • · Hero render uses a wider 96 × 64 viewBox so the dimension brackets fit outside the silhouette.
  • · Single rust callout on the stem (A); everything else is dim ink. Stays restrained.
  • · Drawing numbers and revision tag ("TM · 01", "REV A · 2026") sell the engineering joke without shouting.
  • · Below ~96 px the callouts compress to noise — favicon variant collapses to outline-only.
